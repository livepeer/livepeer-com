# How to create a stream in the Livepeer.com dashboard

If you havenâ€™t already, [create an account on Livepeer.com](/docs/guides/account/create-an-account).

1. Open your Livepeer.com account and navigate to the **streams list page**, [https://livepeer.com/app/user](/app/user).
1. Click the button to create a new stream.
1. On the **create a new stream page**, input a **stream name**.
    - Your stream name needs to be unique and include URL compatible characters only.
1. When you are ready, **save** your stream and go on to the next step.
    - After saving your stream, you will be redirected to the **stream page**.

Next, learn about the [specifications on the stream page](/docs/guides/dashboard/stream-page-specifications).
